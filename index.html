// how we work

A fixed process, in the open.

You'll always know what stage the work is in, what's done, and what's next.

step 01

Scope call

A 30-minute call to understand the problem. If we're not the right fit, we'll say so on the call — not after a proposal.

step 02

Written proposal

Fixed scope, fixed price, and a delivery timeline with buffer built in. What's included is listed; what's excluded is listed too.

step 03

Weekly builds

Working software every week, not a big reveal at the end. You review in your timezone; we work in ours with a 4–5 hour US overlap.

step 04

Handover

Code, documentation, deployment access, and a recorded walkthrough. You own everything. No lock-in, no retainer required.

// straight answer

Why a new studio?

We're early, and we're not going to hide it. No wall of client logos, no invented statistics. What you get instead: the founder writes the code, answers the emails, and is on every call.

That has a real advantage — your project is never handed to a junior team after the sales call, because there is no sales call and no junior team. It also means we take on a small number of projects at a time, and we say no when the fit is wrong.

If you'd rather work with a 200-person agency, that's a reasonable choice. If you want senior attention at a fraction of the cost, email us.
